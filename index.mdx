# Playgent

Playgent is an agent observability platform that automatically detects and clusters problematic traces. You don't need to write a single rubric or eval - Playgent automatically analyzes each trace to detect user struggles, unmet feature needs, agent failures, and more.

## Quick Start

Get started with Playgent in two simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Instrument your agent" icon="code">
    Add the `@record` decorator to create traces and wrap your LLM client - see
    an example in [quickstart](/quickstart)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor your dashboard" icon="chart-line">
    Playgent automatically analyzes your traces and creates incident tickets on
    the [dashboard](https://playgent-dashboard.vercel.app)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Playgent?

Understanding how your AI agent performs in production is critical. Playgent makes this easy by:

- **Automatic issue detection** - No need to write rubrics or evals. Playgent automatically identifies problems in your traces.
- **Intelligent clustering** - Similar issues are grouped together so you can understand patterns at scale
- **Real user insights** - See exactly how users are struggling with your agent
- **Built on OTel standards** - Easily export your traces and spans for further analysis
